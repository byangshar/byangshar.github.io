A Demo Track

Willow + Out of Commission pt. 1

"Willow + Out of Commission pt. 1 are the two original tracks that I worked on for the project 'The Part Timer', since then, the album's direction has taken a drastic shift, with unusually unproductive recording sessions, and an ongoing row of events in my life.
These tracks were discarded for the sake of a more coherent album, and I kept holding onto them until today, when i took upon myself to really release them. The second track, 'Out of Commission', is a spoken, thematic version of a prose that I wrote of the same title.

I am still committed to 'The Part Timer', and plan to record all demos I've worked on since early 2025, so look forward to that."
