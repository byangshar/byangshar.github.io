What Remains of the Hidden (2025)


"What Remains of the Hidden has been in the works for almost a year, longest I've taken to complete a project thus far. I started making the album after my trip to Meghalaya.
Mixing the album was particularly hard, I was learning and experimenting with a lot of new methods and made outright horrible mixes for months!
I've come to however salvage most if not all tracks, and I think they sound decent, fixed according to what capability I had; mixing and mastering was the hardest part of the project overall.
In the meanwhile, I have made a website for everything about music releases and writing that I've delved into recently; a few months went by as i accustomed to that.
I have started working on the next project, I will be publishing tracks of that record soon.
I've unlisted the initial recordings from my main page, you can find them, still, in the playlists tab.
I do realise that this upload has taken far too long. I will be regular going forward, more regular than last year, hopefully!
Thank you all for chiming in!"
