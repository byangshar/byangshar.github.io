"What's this about?"

I made this website initially to make a comprehensive place to showcase all the 'art' that I make. But recently, I've be quite inspired by the creators over at neocities.org. I plan to expand the website (as long as Github keeps me abode) into more extensive writing, accompanied with an archive of all my music releases. I've also planned to write raw commentaries on topics that interest me, sometime in the near future.

Navigation

Home: The landing page of the website, duh.

Releases: This is where all my musical releases are documented. This is still in the works. For the meantime, you can view the archive I made on my previous website.

bandcamp: I uploaded What Remains of the Hidden, my latest album on bandcamp. Buying the Album over there would be the best way to support me. You can also stream the album over there if you have a fued with YouTube for some reason. Thank you for whatever you can do!
